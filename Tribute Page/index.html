Accomplishments

I've stripped the following content from Wikipedia

  • She was a computer scientist and United States Navy Rear Admiral
  • One of the first programmers of the Harvard Mark 1
  • Invented the first compiler for a computer programming language
  • She popularised the idea of machine-independent programming languages
  • She popularised use of the term bug in reference to computer software or hardware design failures
  • She feels the most important thing she did was train young people
  • She was a senior consultant at Digital Equipment Corporation until her death
